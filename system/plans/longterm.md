# Long-Term Plan (3-5 Year Arc)

*Last reasoned: 2026-04-10*

---

## Vision

Build a durable strength base through this 30-week program, then transition toward Olympic lifting. Long-term goal is competence (not necessarily competition) in snatch and clean & jerk.

## Non-Negotiables (Golden Rules)

1. Max 4 training days per week
2. Deload every 5 weeks or when body signals it
3. Never train through sharp joint pain
4. Carb timing around training (insulin resistance context)
